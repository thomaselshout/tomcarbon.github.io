Expedition Nordakar

A long-distance bicycle journey across the continent.

Somewhere between stepping away from work-routine and a desire to keep moving, Expedition Nordkapp took shape. Iâ€™m traveling from the Arctic north of Europe to the heart of West Africa, tracing how landscapes, cultures, and climates slowly transform.

The Stages

The journey unfolds in stages: from the Nordic North, through the forests of the Baltics and Eastern Poland, into the mountains and cultures of the Balkans.

The road then curves along the Mediterranean and climbs across the Atlas Mountains. From there, it stretches into the Sahara, on the long way to Dakar.

With every stage, the climate changes, the food evolves and the rhythm of daily life shifts.
